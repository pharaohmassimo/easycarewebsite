Our Story

Health Care Made Easy

Easy Care Suppliers Ltd was set up by a group of highly qualified Nurses and HCA’s with over 20 years of experience working within the NHS hospitals and other private Hospitals as a way of working as a team saving the health care sector being one of their supplier. We are experts, nurse-led recruitment teams, who are highly experienced with exceptional clinical and leadership skills. We’re therefore best placed to recruit like-minded professionals to represent Easy Care name
